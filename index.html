Northwest Credit Union Association (NWCUA) is the premier trade association representing more than 170 credit unions in Idaho, Oregon, and Washington with associate members in Alaska and Hawaii. As the NWCUA and Northwest credit unions adapt to a new operating and engagement environment in the midst of COVID-19, new opportunities for valuable visibility & strategic alignment are emerging in the digital space.

The NWCUA continues to act as the unifying voice for credit unions with state and federal legislators; helps credit unions shoulder a weighty compliance burden; provides abundant, professional development opportunities; and partners with our members to communicate the Credit Union Difference in local and national media. Expanded programming and meetings are evolving in virtual formats as the Association continues to be a resource and convener for Northwest credit unions. This support helps Northwest credit unions of all sizes thrive for their 7.3 million members.
